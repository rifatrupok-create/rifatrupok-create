About

Driven by
impact.

With 5+ years of progressive experience across fundraising, grant management, and community development, I bring a research-informed, equity-centred approach to development work. Based in Winnipeg, I am deeply connected to the city's non-profit and social sector landscape.


I hold an MSS in Peace and Conflict Studies from the University of Dhaka and a Fundraising & Development Foundations certificate from UC Davis. My work sits at the intersection of data, relationships, and social change.


Beyond my development role, I am an active public scholar — publishing on South Asian geopolitics, India-Bangladesh relations, and social movements. My SSRN papers have accumulated over 3,000 abstract views internationally.
